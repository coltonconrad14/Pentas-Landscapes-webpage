Our Story

Rooted in Austin,
Growing With You

Pentas Landscapes was born from a simple belief: that beautiful outdoor spaces strengthen communities. Named after the cheerful, resilient pentas flower — a Texas garden favorite — we bring that same energy to every lawn, garden, and neighborhood we serve.

Based in the greater Austin area, we understand Central Texas like no one else. We know the soil, the heat, the Hill Country winds, and the native plants that make this region uniquely beautiful. Our team combines horticultural expertise with genuine care for every client.

Local Expertise

Deep knowledge of Austin's climate, soils, and native plants.

Community-Focused

We're neighbors first — invested in Austin's growth and beauty.

Exceptional Quality

Every detail matters — from the first blade of grass to the final bloom.
